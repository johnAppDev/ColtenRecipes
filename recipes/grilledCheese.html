Grilled Cheese

Grilled cheese is an easy to make lunch. It is delicious but not particularly nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • cheese, as much as you want
  • butter

How to make:

  1. Grab 2 slices of bread and put cheese in between them
  2. Slice butter and put the slices on the bread on the outside
  3. Turn on the stove, and put the sandwich on a pan. Then put the pan on the grill.
  4. Grill until it is golden brown on one side and then flip
  5. Once it is golden brown on the other side, take the sandwich from the pan and serve.
