Toast

Toast is a delicious and easy to prepare snack

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 slices of sliced bread
  • butter

How to make:

  1. Take out two slices of bread
  2. Put them into the toaster
  3. Push down the little handle thingy, and pray that you have the toaster on the right setting
  4. Listen for a pop sound
  5. Once you hear the sound, head over to the toaster and remove the toast from the apparatus
  6. Apply butter(as much or as little as you would like) by cutting it from the stick and spreading it on the toasted bread
