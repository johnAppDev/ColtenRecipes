Peanut Butter and Jelly

Peanut Butter and Jelly is an easy to make lunch/snack that is also quite delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of sliced bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

How to make:

  1. Take out two slices of bread
  2. apply peanut butter to one piece with a butter knife
  3. Apply Jelly to the other piece by using a spoon
  4. Put the two slices together and enjoy.
